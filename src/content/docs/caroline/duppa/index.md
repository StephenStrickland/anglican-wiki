Brian Duppa
1588-1662

Dean of Christ Church;
Bishop successively of Chichester, Salisbury and Winchester

> Two Prayers, one for the safety of His Majesties Person: the other for the preservation of this university and city of Oxford: to be used in all churches and chappells.
>
> [Private Forms of Prayer Fit for Sad Times](/caroline/duppa/priv/):
> Heretofore Printed at Oxford, and used (occasionally) upon Dayes of Solemn Humiliation and Fasting, in his late Majesties Royall Chappell of blessed and glorious Memory.
> Also A Form of Prayer for the 30th of January, Morning and Evening. Likewise Prayers of Intercession for the Use of such as Mourn in Secret.
> Together with a Collection of severall other Prayers.
> London: 1660.
>
> [A Guide for the Penitent](/caroline/duppa/penitent/), or,
> A modell drawn up for the help of a devout soul wounded with sin
> London: Printed by J. Fletcher, for R. Royston, 1664.
